Site Plan

PopCorn Picks

This name represents popcorn as the classic movie snack, while “picks” highlights the idea of curated selections. Together, PopCornPicks conveys a fun and inviting place where people can discover and share movie recommendations that everyone can enjoy.

Site Purpose

The site provides many movie picks from the people who visited the site and some I added.

Scenarios

  • What's a good light comedy I can watch this weekend to relax?
  • What are the top-rated sci-fi movies currently available on streaming platforms?
